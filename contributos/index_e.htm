    Initial considerations

         When an article is submitted to Intervir, the author is accepting that its format may be changed, in order to correspond to the guidelines defined by the journal editors.

         The decisions regarding the publishing of articles in the Intervir journal will be made by the Editors. Submitted articles, even if they aren't selected to be published,  will not be returned.

     

    General information

         The articles submitted to the Intervir journal may assume two different formats: 

      Long article: Article with scientific, educational or academic purposes. Text size: between 3000 and 7000 words.

      Short article: Opinion , review, critiques. Text size: up to 2500 words.

         Whatever the chosen format, the text must be sent in RTF, DOC or HTML file type. Whenever the file size exceeds 200Kb it would be convenient to compress it before sending by e-mail.

For further information, please contact the Editors.

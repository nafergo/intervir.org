Long Articles 

 

    Content     Articles should have scientific, educational or academic purposes and respect the framework defined by the editorial guidelines.
         The articles can be theorical approaches, with substantial review  of literature from the field, or reports of empirical approaches, research, methodological grounded.
    Language     Assume familiarity with the terminology of the discipline, with scientific terms and language.
    Authors     Specialists, experts, researchers. Authors should have relevant background (knowledge, expertise) in the field.
    Sources and References     We strongly recommend authors to list used sources and references. The author should follow the references and text format rules identified bellow.
    Graphics     The author may use pictures, tables, charts or other graphics if they're relevant, support the text, help to explain the article content.
    Text size     Between 3000 and 7000 words.

     

    Text format:

    • Title: Arial, size 18, Bold, center.

    • Author identification: The author's name should appear in the line immediately after the title. Arial, size 10, Bold, center

    • Abstract: Times New Roman, size 10, line spacing simple. Up to 250 words.

    • Paragraph: There should be one blank line between consecutive paragraphs.

    • Text size: Times New Roman, size12, paragraphs aligned left. Line spacing simple.

    • Subtitles inside the text (Headings): 

      • There should be one blank line after each heading and two blank lines before each heading.

      • Nível 1 - Times New Roman, size 14, bold, aligned left.

      • Nível 2 - Times New Roman, size 12, bold, aligned left.

      • Nível 3 - Times New Roman, size 12, italic, aligned left.

    • References cited in the article: Must be identified with " in the beginning and at the end.

    • Author presentation: After the end of the article, there should be a text (no more than 2 paragraphs, about 20 lines) presenting the author of the article (published articles and books, research interests, Institution or Organization where the author works, etc).

 

    Other aspects related to text format:

    • To highlight text use italic or bold.

    • The author may insert bookmarks and hyperlinks inside the article.

    • Tables and graphics must be centered horizontally.

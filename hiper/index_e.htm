VREL - Virtual Reality and Education Laboratory

   The VREL is a laboratory from the School of Education, East Carolina University, that studies the educational use and potential of VR. It's directed by Dr. Lawrence Auld and Drª Veronica Pantelidis and you have free access to an electronic version of their journal: VR in the Schools.
   If you're interested, we recommend that you pay a visit to our number 1 issue (September 2001), where you can find an article written by Dr. Auld.

World Social Forum

   From the 31 of January until the 5 of February 2002, Porto Alegre, Brasil, will host another World Social Forum. The WSF site has useful informationsto those who want to travel to Porto Alegre, an agenda of events and several texts presenting ideas and proposals for a different world.
   If you're interested, we recommend that you pay a visit to our number 1 issue (September 2001), where you can find the speech that Drª. Susan George send to the last year WSF (2001).
